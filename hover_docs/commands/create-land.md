Creates a single non-player (neutral) land.
May be assigned to a specific player with [assign_to_player](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit#heading=h.mok24wym6kiz) or [assign_to](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit#heading=h.b6uul7n11c6g).

  land_percent 10
