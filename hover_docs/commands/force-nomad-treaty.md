```rms
force_nomad_treaty
```

---

Activates a treaty period on nomad maps (maps without a starting Town Center).
The treaty lasts until one of the following occurs:
- Every player constructs a Town Center.
- 5&nbsp;minutes of game time elapses.

The border of the treaty is visible in explored tiles.
Most tournaments forego the treaty in favor of rules to prevent early Town Center and Villager fighting.

**Example**:

```rms
<PLAYER_SETUP>
nomad_resources
force_nomad_treaty
```
