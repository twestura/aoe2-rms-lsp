<CLIFF_GENERATION>

Place cliffs and adjust cliff settings.

- Omit this section for maps without cliffs.
- Cliffs avoid the origins of all lands by 22 tiles, do not spawn on water terrains, and do not spawn on slopes.
- The terrain `ROCK1` spawns under cliffs. This terrian is present during [<TERRAIN_GENERATION>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit#heading=h.2mwmqwe7m0vw) and [<CONNECTION_GENERATION>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.urxh5ze1aaoh), but is replaced by the map's [base_terrain](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.t4mfjnozvxwf) before [<OBJECTS_GENERATION>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jnhZXoeL9mkRUJxcGlKnO98fIwFKStP_OBozpr0CHXo/edit#heading=h.unkzqmnnqr5v).
